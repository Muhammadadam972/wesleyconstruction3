Who We Are

Wesley Construction and Supply Nigeria Limited was incorporated on July 7, 2024, under the Companies and Allied Matters Act 2020, with RC No. 7657977. The company officially commenced operations on January 1, 2024, with its head office in Abuja and a branch in Kano. Since inception, Wesley Construction has positioned itself as a reliable partner in construction and supply services, providing innovative solutions across building, road construction, estate development, and material supplies. Despite being a young company, Wesley has demonstrated growth and compliance, achieving significant turnover within its first year of business operations.
